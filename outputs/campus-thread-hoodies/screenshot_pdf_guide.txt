CampusThread Hoodies - Suggested Screenshot PDF Contents

Use your teacher's official PDF template when posted. These are the recommended screenshots and short descriptions:

1. Home Page
   Shows the Group 4 CampusThread logo, group name, hero section, featured hoodie products, and footer disclaimer.

2. Buyer Registration Page
   Shows complete name, valid email, password, confirm password, complete address, and contact number fields.

3. Email Confirmation Page or Confirmation Message
   Shows that registration creates a confirmation step before login.

4. Store Page
   Shows categorized university hoodie products and add to cart buttons.

5. Cart Page
   Shows selected products, editable quantities, subtotal, delivery fee, and checkout button.

6. Checkout Page
   Shows delivery details form with customer name, email, address, and contact numbers.

7. Payment Page
   Shows manual payment choices without using a payment API.

8. Seller Admin Dashboard
   Shows seller statistics and navigation to user, stock, and report pages.

9. User Management Page
   Shows users and roles that the system admin can add or modify.

10. Product Stock Management Page
    Shows hoodie stock, prices, status, and modify links.

11. Reports Page
    Shows remaining inventory and audit log report.

12. About Page
    Shows company information and group members.
